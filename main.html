This bedroom, that has uninterrupted views of the vast sea, is drenched in natural light the whole day. The ample use of wood and leather lend warmth to the space. The mid-century furniture add to the detailing in this minimalist space.

The architect has used unique artworks, light fixtures, furniture patterns and materials to create a one-of-a-kind space. This bedroom for the client's daughter is luxurious and eclectic. The space features a multi-coloured painted timber wall which looks straight out of a fairytale.		 The four-poster bed, wooden flooring and cabinets help create a soothing cocoon. Against the deep tones, the teal bedding and the wall behind the bed, pop out. The light adds a transient element to the design as the hours on the clock tick and add a touch of drama.
Matte blues juxtapose with whites and a serene, smart and sumptuous interior emerges. The master bedroom is accentuated with intriguing elements, be it the pendant lighting, the curios or the photographs. The wooden flooring anchors the different hues in the room.
